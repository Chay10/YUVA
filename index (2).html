ABOUT US

We are pleased to announce "Yuva", the Women Innovators Challenge, a 24-hour womenâ€™s ideathon is an event designed to bring together women from diverse backgrounds to collaborate and generate innovative ideas to solve societal challenges that affect women. During the ideathon, participants will engage in brainstorming sessions, idea pitching, and team formation to develop an idea for their solution.

The event can focus on various themes, such as women's health, education, financial empowerment, gender equality, and more. To make the ideathon successful, participants are provided with access to mentors, sessions, and resources needed to turn their ideas into a reality.

At the end of the 24-hour ideathon, teams will present their solutions to a panel of judges who will evaluate their prototypes based on the originality, feasibility, potential impact, clarity, and scalability of the idea. The winning team will receive recognition, prizes, and incubation support to develop their solution further.

The ideathon provides an excellent opportunity for women to collaborate, learn, and showcase their creativity and problem-solving skills. It also creates a platform for networking and building connections that can lead to new opportunities for personal and professional growth.
